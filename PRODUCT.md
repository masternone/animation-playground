# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Designers and frontend developers exploring browser animation techniques, comparing motion implementations, and looking for focused examples they can learn from.

## Product Purpose

Animation Playground is a visual showcase for expressive web-motion experiments. Each page should make its animation concept immediately understandable while giving experiments enough space to feel vivid and memorable.

## Brand Personality

Cheerful, confident, loud.

## Anti-references

Muted corporate sites, generic SaaS landing pages, and dashboard-like interfaces that make the experiments feel administrative or overly restrained.

## Design Principles

- Let each motion experiment be the main event.
- Make navigation obvious without competing with the work.
- Use bold, deliberate visual choices rather than decorative clutter.
- Keep comparisons fair and concepts easy to understand.
- Preserve fast loading and responsive interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA, support reduced-motion preferences, preserve strong keyboard focus states and semantic structure, and maximize Lighthouse accessibility, performance, best-practices, and SEO scores.
